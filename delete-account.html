Account Deletion Policy - Kontext

Important: This page outlines how users can request deletion of their Kontext account and associated data.

How to Delete Your Account

You can request deletion of your Kontext account through the following methods:

Method 1: In-App Settings

  1. Open the Kontext app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Scroll down to "Account Management"
  4. Select "Delete Account"
  5. Follow the confirmation prompts

Method 2: Email Request

Send an email to: mokshworkspace@gmail.com

Include in your email:

  • Subject: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Your registered email address
  • Reason for deletion (optional)

What Data Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your profile information and settings
  • Chat history and conversations
  • Personal preferences and customizations
  • Usage analytics tied to your account
  • Any stored authentication tokens
Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Please ensure you have backed up any important conversations or data before proceeding.

Data Retention

  • Immediate deletion: Most personal data is deleted immediately upon request
  • 30-day grace period: Some backup data may be retained for up to 30 days for recovery purposes
  • Legal requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law or for legitimate business purposes

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are typically processed within:

  • In-app deletion: Immediate to 24 hours
  • Email requests: 3-5 business days

What Happens After Deletion

  • You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete
  • You will no longer be able to access your account
  • All associated data will be permanently removed
  • You may create a new account at any time with the same email address

Contact Information

If you have questions about account deletion or need assistance:

Email: support@kontext.app

Response time: Within 48 hours

Privacy Policy

For more information about how we handle your data, please review our Privacy Policy.
